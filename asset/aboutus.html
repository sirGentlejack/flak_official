About Us

Flak skill academy is a community foundation for the less privileged in Nigeria. The vision of the academy is to ensure a poverty free Nigeria by moving Nigerians from poverty to plenty. The academy intends to achieve its mission by providing food banks, scholarships, skill acquisition, ethics and value reorientation programs and volunteering programs for the youth and less privileged people in Nigeria. The academy will gradually spread its impacts across 36 states in the first five years, however in the first year (2021), the foundation shall commence with 10 pilot key states and continue to expand gradually. The impact the academy intends to achieve is to have 324 scholarship beneficiaries, 360 skill acquisition and empowerment beneficiaries, to provide 4,128,000 plates of food to food bank beneficiaries. It also intends to conduct orientation program for 1% of the population and support to volunteers for community development.
